# Certifications Filter - User Guide

## What is the Certifications Filter?

The Certifications filter helps job seekers find positions that require or prefer specific industry certifications. This is especially useful for energy sector professionals who have invested in certifications like OSHA, API, CDL, or maritime licenses.

## How to Use

### 1. Access the Filter
- Navigate to the Jobs page
- Look for the "Certifications" section in the left sidebar filters
- Below Company, Location, and Skills filters

### 2. Search for Certifications
- Click the certification dropdown
- Type to search (e.g., "CDL", "OSHA", "Six Sigma")
- Select one or multiple certifications
- Selected certifications appear as blue pills in the dropdown

### 3. Filter Jobs
- Jobs are filtered to show only those mentioning selected certifications
- Uses OR logic: job must have at least ONE of the selected certifications
- Count updates to show how many jobs match

### 4. Clear Filters
- Click "Clear all" at the top of the filters section
- Or click the X on individual certification pills in the dropdown

3. Identify jobs where your certifications meet requirements

- Use with Location filter to find certified positions near you

## Data Source

Certifications are automatically extracted from:
- Job description text
- Skills/requirements sections
- Qualification statements

The system uses pattern matching to identify certification mentions and normalizes variations to standard names.
